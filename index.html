Designers

Here are two designers/brands that I take heavy inspiration from and deeply admire. I've alwasy been fascinated and enraptured by their work. I love the aesthetics that these desginers and brands embody, and hope to one day populate my closet with a multitude of pieces by them.

Jean Paul Gautier

A worldwide renowned French fashion designer, Jean Paul Gautier is known for pushing the boundaries of fashion and challenging conventions. His work is marked by unconventional silhouettes, reimagining classic garments with unexpected twists and blurring gender boundaries thorugh hyper-androgny. He incoporates unexpected materials, such as newspaper and plastic bags (seen in his 2010 couture collection). He also utilizes contrasing elements, blending high and low fashion, luxury and streetwear, and traditional and contemporary influences.

Vivienne Westwood

A famous English fashion designer, Vivienne Westwood embodies a bold, fearless, and rebellious style throughout her work. She takes heavy inspiration from historical fashion, parodying English looks, creating innovative takes on traditional tailoring, and making sexually charged revivals of past looks. She also has a heavy punk influence in her designs, with unconventional silhouettes, distressed fabrics, and motifs like safety pins, tartan prints, and bondage inspired details. She is known for her corsets and use of tartan, and remains an alternative and creative force in fashion.
