y2k aesthetic

Overview

y2k refers to the style and trends associated with 1990s and 2000s fashion (y2k = year 2000). This aesthetic encapsulates a playful, experimental, and daring style. It combines a mix of futuristic and retro elements, and is known for breaking rules, mixing patterns, and rebellion. This style incorporates shiny tones, reflective metallics, mixtures of textures, and unconventional combinations of pieces. This aesthetic is heavily driven by pop culture and takes heavy streetwear inspiration. Key clothing items include low rise silhouettes, bedazzled detailing, cargos, tube tops, and loud colors and graphics. Some may even call y2k where streetwear meets glamour and excess.
