Maximalist Aesthetic

Overview

Maximalism is also a concept, in addition to an aesthetic and style. It emboldens the "more is more" philosophy, characterized by unashamed excess and exuberance. It is the epitome of opulence and audacity, with statement pieces, color designs, clashing textures, mixed patterns, vibrant colors, and intense layering. Fashion wise, there is a heavy influence of 1990s and 2000s fashion, with its experimentalist and rebellious nature. Personally, I am most inspired by maximalist jewelry and accessorizing for my own personal style and how I incorporate this aesthetic to be my own.
